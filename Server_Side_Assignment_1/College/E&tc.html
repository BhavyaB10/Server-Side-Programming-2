Electronic & Telecommunication Engineering

About this course

Electronics and Telecommunication Engineering is a branch of engineering that deals with the study of electronic devices, circuits, and communication equipment. It focuses on the design, development, testing, and maintenance of communication systems.

Iet offers both Bachelors' and Masters' program for Computer Engineering.
