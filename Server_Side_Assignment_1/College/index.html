    About IET DAVV, Indore

    Established in 1996, IET DAVV (Institute of Engineering and Technology, Devi Ahilya Vishwavidyalaya) is a premier engineering college located in Indore, Madhya Pradesh. Affiliated with Devi Ahilya Vishwavidyalaya, IET DAVV offers a range of undergraduate and postgraduate programs in various engineering disciplines

    Academic Programs

    IET DAVV offers Bachelor of Engineering (B.E.) programs in disciplines such as Computer Science and Engineering, Mechanical Engineering, Electrical Engineering, Civil Engineering, and Electronics and Communication Engineering. The college also offers Master of Engineering (M.E.) programs in several specializations.

    Placements

    IET DAVV has a dedicated placement cell that works closely with industry partners to facilitate internships and job placements for students. The college has a strong track record of placements, with students securing placements in leading companies in India.

    Extracurricular Activities

    In addition to academics, IET DAVV encourages students to participate in various extracurricular activities such as sports, cultural events, and technical competitions. These activities help in the overall development of students and provide them with opportunities to showcase their talents.
